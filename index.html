Introduction

Hi! My name is Jenny. I'm a linguist turned front-end web developer. For the past 7 years I've worked at local agencies on B2B and non-profit projects. See some of my recent projects.

I'm a thoughtful and resourceful person with a mind for creative problem-solving. Continuing to learn is important to me and I love figuring out how things work. That's why I was drawn to this industry.

Where are you from?

I was born and reared in the heart of the Willamette Valley, aka Corvallis. I live in Portland now. I've spent some time in other places nearby like Vancouver (BC), San Juan Island, and other places farther away like Tokyo.
